About

Aspiring maverick designer, tweaking perfectionist, lover of all things art and creative, and shark enthusiast.

Born and raised in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Currently attending Algonquin College for Graphic Design; graduating Spring 2016. Trained in many areas; specialized in website design, packaging, and print.

Inspirations include—but not limited to—nature and animals, food and culture, graffiti, comics and superheroes, cars, music, movies, and designer vinyl toys.
